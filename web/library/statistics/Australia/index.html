Trends in infant circumcision
=============================

The number and rate reached record annual lows in Tasmania and NT
in February.

Rate is Medicare claims for infant circumcision as a percentage of
male births.

Vertical line shows range of rate during period Jul 1993 to Feb 2004.
Indicator (^ = Up, > = Sideways or v = Down) shows rate at Feb 2004
and current trend of rate.

Chart 2 shows that the rate is heading further down in the states
which already have the lowest rates (the three states at the left of
the chart -- Tas, NT, and ACT), trending sideways in WA, SA and Qld,
and rising in Vic and NSW.
