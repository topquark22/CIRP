Legality of Circumcision: The United States of America

This page indexes and links discussions of the position of male and female circumcision under the law in the United States of America.

In December 2004, Edwin Bruce Baxter was convicted in Clark County, Washington of second degree child assault because he attempted to circumcise his eight-year-old son (State v. Edwin Bruce Baxter, case no. 04-1-01764-5.) He was sentenced to serve 36 months in jail.23
