SOUTH AFRICAN BROADCASTING CORPORATION, Johannesburg,
30 July 2001.


New circumcision laws in pipeline

Johannesburg - A new law to regulate traditional initiation and circumcision procedures is in the pipeline after 250 boys attending initiation schools were hospitalised and 35 died, SABC television news reported on Sunday.

This year's initiation season has now ended.

In the wake of a trail of botched mutilations, the Health Department has vowed to take full control of the carrying out of these customary procedures before the next initiation season begins.
